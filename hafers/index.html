Welcome to Hafer's Gunsmithing

Hafer’s gunsmithing is your one stop shop for all your firearms needs in the tri-state area! We buy, sell, trade, consign, repair & customize all firearms including NFA/CLASS III.

Since 2000 Hafer’s custom gunsmithing has been above precision; beyond accurate! Detail, training, and experience will prove our commitment from the finest custom gun to the common repair.

Whether its your first time firearm purchase, a custom gun of a lifetime, NFA/Class III, or you need training; Hafer’s professional staff are trained, knowledgeable and experienced which sets us above the rest!

Stop in our show room or shop online! Hafer’s is here to help!
